History Of the Sneaker

Yeezy is a clothing and footwear brand created by American designer, rapper and entrepreneur Kanye West . Currently, the main products of Yeezy are released as part of a collaboration with the German sportswear company Adidas . The collaboration was made famous by high-end limited edition Yeezy Boost sneakers . T-shirts, jackets, sweatpants, socks, slippers, women's shoes and slippers were also released as part of the collaboration. The first shoe model ( Yeezy Boost 750 ) was released in February 2015 [1] .
